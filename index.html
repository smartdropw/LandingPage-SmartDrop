Frequently asked questions

Can I install the sensors myself? ▼

Yes, all sensor installations are designed to be quick and easy. Detailed guides are provided, and most setups can be done without specialized skills.

Is my data secure in the cloud? ▼

Absolutely. Your data security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption protocols for data transmission and storage.

How do the alerts work? ▼

Alerts are customizable based on volume, pressure, or temperature thresholds you define. Notifications can be sent via email, SMS, or within the dashboard.
